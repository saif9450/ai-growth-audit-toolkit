# Growth Audit Framework

A structured system to diagnose and fix growth problems in startups and D2C brands.

---

## 1. Traffic Quality

- Are we attracting the right audience?
- Source breakdown (paid, organic, referral)
- Intent vs actual offering match

---

## 2. Funnel Efficiency

- Landing page conversion rate
- Drop-off points
- User journey clarity

---

## 3. Offer Strength

- Is the value proposition clear?
- Is it differentiated?
- Is there urgency?

---

## 4. Creative & Messaging

- Hook effectiveness
- Clarity of communication
- Emotional vs logical appeal

---

## 5. Unit Economics (Basic Layer)

- CAC vs AOV
- Contribution margin
- Payback period (if data available)

---

## 6. Retention & LTV

- Repeat purchase rate
- Customer experience
- Retention systems (email, WhatsApp, etc.)

---

## 7. Key Bottleneck Identification

Identify the ONE biggest constraint:
- Traffic problem?
- Conversion problem?
- Offer problem?

---

## 8. Action Plan

### Quick Wins
- Low effort, high impact fixes

### Strategic Moves
- Bigger structural improvements

---

## Philosophy

Growth is not about doing more.
It’s about fixing the biggest constraint first.
